SMARTDESK PRODUCT FAQ

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely.

Q: Do you offer warranties on products?
A: Yes. All products come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty. Some products have extended warranties of 2-3 years. Check the product page for specific warranty information.

Q: Can I buy products in bulk?
A: Yes, we offer bulk discounts for orders of 10 or more units. Contact our sales team at sales@smartdesk.com for bulk pricing.

Q: Are your products available internationally?
A: We currently ship to the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?
A: Yes, gift wrapping is available for $3.99 per item. You can add a personalized message during checkout.

Q: How do I know if a product is in stock?
A: Product availability is shown on each product page. If an item is out of stock, you can sign up for a restock notification by clicking "Notify Me".

Q: Do you price match?
A: Yes, we offer price matching for identical products sold by authorized retailers. Contact support with the competitor's URL and we will match the price.
